# ChefMind

ChefMind is your intelligent nutrition companion powered by AI. Take a photo or describe what you ate, and Gemini AI instantly analyzes the nutritional content including calories, macronutrients, vitamins, and minerals.

• AI Food Analysis — Snap a photo or type a description to get instant nutritional breakdown
• Personalized Weekly Menu — AI generates a 7-day meal plan tailored to your profile, health conditions, allergies, and preferences
• Nutrient Tracking — Monitor vitamins, minerals, and macros with RDA-based daily targets
• Shopping List — Automatically consolidates ingredients from your weekly menu
• HealthKit Integration — Import age, gender, weight, and height from Health
• Smart Meal Diary — Log meals and track consumption day by day
• Premium — Unlock unlimited menu generation, photo analysis, and dish replacement

Whether you're managing dietary goals, exploring new cuisines, or just curious about what's in your food, ChefMind makes nutritional analysis effortless.
